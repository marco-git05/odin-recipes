Vanilla Crepes

Description

This vanilla crepe recipe creates thin, delicate pancakes with a light, sweet flavor. Perfectly versatile, these crepes can be filled with a variety of toppings like fresh fruit, whipped cream, or Nutella, making them a delicious choice for breakfast, brunch, or dessert. Easy to make and sure to impress, they are a simple yet elegant treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Milk (1 1/2 cups)
  • Egg Yolks (3)
  • Vanilla extract (2 tablespoons)
  • All-purpose flour (1 1/2 cups)
  • Sugar (2 tablespoons)
  • Salt (1/2 teaspoons)
  • Melted butter (5 tablespoons)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix milk, egg yolks, and vanilla. Stir in the flour, sugar, salt, and melted butter until well blended.
  2. Heat a crepe pan over medium heat until hot. Coat with vegetable oil or cooking spray.
  3. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the pan and tip to spread the batter to the edges.
  4. Flip over and cook until lightly browned on the other side and edges are golden.
  5. Fill crepes with desired fruits, cream, or caramel and serve.
