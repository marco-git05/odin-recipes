Banana Pancakes

Description

Banana pancakes are a quick and easy breakfast option that can serve many. Their minimal prep time and simple ingredients make this recipe a must-try for anyone who loves pancakes or is a fan of breakfast in general. Perfect for busy mornings or weekend brunches, these pancakes are both delicious and satisfying, offering a great way to enjoy a classic breakfast with a healthy twist.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (1 cup)
  • White sugar (1 tablespoon)
  • Baking powder (2 teaspoons)
  • Salt (1/4 teaspoon)
  • Beaten eggs (1)
  • Milk (1 cup)
  • Vegetable oil (2 tablespoons)
  • Mashed ripe bananas (2)

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Combine flour, white sugar, baking powder, and salt into a bowl.
  3. Mix together egg, milk, vegetable oil, and bananas in a second bowl.
  4. Stir the flour mixture into banana mixture.
  5. Heat a lighlt oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  6. Cook pancakes until golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes per side. Serve hot.
